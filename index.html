Welcome!

Hi, my name is Ben Kolera and I am a Full-Stack Web Developer in love with the safety and composability of Functional Programming. You can find more about me and my story, here.

The goal of this blog is to share my thoughts and the message that Functional Programming gives a lot of wins even for stock standard web programming. You do not need to be a computer scientist to appreciate Haskell!! More on why I love Haskell here.

I also co-organise the Brisbane FP Group and speak there semi-regularly, so I'll also record my talks here.
